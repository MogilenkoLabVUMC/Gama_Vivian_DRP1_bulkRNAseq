===============================================================================
NEURONAL MATURATION PATHWAY FILTERING STRATEGY
===============================================================================

Based on inspection of raw CSV files, the following non-neuronal tissue-specific
pathways should be EXCLUDED from visualizations:

TISSUE-SPECIFIC EXCLUSIONS:
---------------------------
1. Pancreas & Endocrine:
   - HALLMARK_PANCREAS_BETA_CELLS
   - GOBP_PANCREAS_DEVELOPMENT
   - REACTOME_REGULATION_OF_GENE_EXPRESSION_IN_BETA_CELLS
   - Keywords: pancreas, beta_cell, insulin (except insulin signaling)

2. Cardiac & Cardiovascular:
   - SIG_PIP3_SIGNALING_IN_CARDIAC_MYOCTES
   - GOBP_CARDIAC_CHAMBER_MORPHOGENESIS
   - GOBP_CARDIAC_SEPTUM_MORPHOGENESIS
   - GOBP_CARDIAC_MUSCLE_TISSUE_DEVELOPMENT
   - GOBP_CARDIAC_NEURAL_CREST (keep - relevant to neural)
   - Keywords: cardiac, heart, myocardium, cardiomyocyte

3. Kidney & Renal:
   - GOBP_KIDNEY_EPITHELIUM_DEVELOPMENT
   - GOBP_KIDNEY_MORPHOGENESIS
   - Keywords: kidney, renal, nephron

4. Gastrointestinal:
   - GOBP_INTESTINAL_EPITHELIAL_CELL_DIFFERENTIATION
   - Keywords: intestin, colon, gut, digestive

5. Respiratory:
   - GOBP_LUNG_MORPHOGENESIS
   - GOBP_LUNG_EPITHELIUM_DEVELOPMENT
   - Keywords: lung, alveolar, bronchial

6. Other Non-Neuronal:
   - HALLMARK_ADIPOGENESIS (fat/adipose)
   - HALLMARK_ANDROGEN_RESPONSE (hormonal)
   - HALLMARK_BILE_ACID_METABOLISM (liver)
   - HALLMARK_COAGULATION (blood clotting)
   - HALLMARK_XENOBIOTIC_METABOLISM (drug metabolism)
   - HALLMARK_HEME_METABOLISM (blood)
   - HALLMARK_MYOGENESIS (muscle, except neuromuscular)
   - HALLMARK_UV_RESPONSE (skin)

KEEP (NEURONAL-RELEVANT):
-------------------------
1. Neuron-Specific:
   - Anything with: neuro, neural, neuron, synap, axon, dendrite, brain
   - SynGO database (all)

2. General Cell Biology (relevant to neurons):
   - Cell cycle: E2F_TARGETS, G2M_CHECKPOINT
   - Mitochondria: OXIDATIVE_PHOSPHORYLATION
   - Protein synthesis: Ribosome, translation
   - Cell death: APOPTOSIS, P53_PATHWAY
   - Stress response: HYPOXIA, DNA_REPAIR
   - Signaling: MYC_TARGETS, TNFA_SIGNALING_VIA_NFKB
   - Immune (can affect neurons): INTERFERON, INFLAMMATORY_RESPONSE

3. Development (keep if not tissue-specific):
   - EMT (epithelial-mesenchymal transition)
   - General developmental signaling

FILTERING APPROACH:
-------------------
1. Create exclusion regex patterns for non-neuronal tissues
2. Create inclusion regex patterns for neuronal/brain terms
3. For ambiguous pathways, check if they contain neuronal inclusion terms
4. Apply hierarchical filtering: exclude first, then re-include neuronal hits
